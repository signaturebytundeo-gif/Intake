About InTake Impact Corp

Building a community-driven support system for those who served our nation

Our Mission

We firmly believe that meaningful impact begins with the individuals we serve. Our mission is to support U.S. veterans and Gold Star families by developing programs and systems that facilitate mutual aid, with a strong emphasis on raising awareness through community outreach efforts.

Our Approach

InTake Impact Corp goes beyond the traditional charity model. We maintain operations through in-house marketing and the Most Valuable Players (MVPs) public service announcement campaigns, producing impactful print, audio, and film PSAs that support our donors, sponsors, and community.

Our Impact

Together, we are creating a system that enables communities to establish mutual aid for U.S. veterans and Gold Star families. We believe sharing resourcesregardless of their formcan eliminate barriers and unlock opportunities that humility can present to society.

MOST VALUABLE PLAYERS / U.S. VETERANS

U.S. Veterans Are The MVPs Among American Citizens

Our MVPs campaign acknowledges the bravery, dedication, and sacrifices of those who served, while also recognizing Gold Star families who have lost loved ones in the line of duty.

Why Veterans Matter

Sacrifice and Service

Veterans have dedicated years of their lives to protecting our freedoms and way of life. Many have faced danger, hardship, and separation from loved ones in service to our nation.

Transition Challenges

Returning to civilian life presents unique challenges including employment, housing, healthcare access, and mental health support. Our programs address these critical needs.

Community Support

Creating strong community networks ensures veterans and Gold Star families never feel alone. Through mutual aid and outreach, we build lasting support systems.

Gold Star Families

Gold Star families have made the ultimate sacrifice, losing loved ones in service to our country. They deserve our unwavering support, recognition, and assistance in honoring their fallen heroes.

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to support, empower, and honor our veterans and Gold Star families

MVPs PSA Campaign

Our flagship awareness initiative featuring print, audio, and film public service announcements led by Ambassador Mitch 'The Rock' Richmond. Through innovative media campaigns, we raise awareness that U.S. veterans truly are the Most Valuable Players among American citizens.

Impact: Reaching millions through multi-platform campaigns

Led by: NBA Hall of Famer Mitch Richmond

InTake Lifestyle: Health & Wellness

Dedicated to enhancing the mental and physical well-being of U.S. veterans and Gold Star families. We collaborate with trainers, psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers, and advocates to offer workshops, coaching, exercise programs, healthy eating initiatives, and counseling sessions.

Services: Mental health counseling, fitness programs, nutrition guidance

Partners: Licensed professionals and certified coaches

Learn More

Volunteer & Internship Program

A college-credited initiative where volunteers are the heart of our organization. Their selfless acts and commitment to supporting veterans, armed forces, and Gold Star families demonstrate that service starts at home. We build awareness and provide community outreach that creates mutual aid.

Benefits: College credit, hands-on experience, community impact

Opportunities: Event support, outreach, program coordination

Learn More

Veterans Employment Initiative

Creating meaningful employment opportunities for veterans through partnerships with InTake Global & Beverages operations, including fulfillment centers, manufacturing facilities, and arena concessions. We're putting our veterans to work in dignified, impactful roles.

Positions: Manufacturing, logistics, operations, management

Benefits: Competitive pay, career advancement, skills training
